# Setting Up Your Profile

Your profile is your digital presence on Eleva Care. A complete, professional profile helps patients find you and builds trust before they book.

## Profile Elements

<Tabs items={['Required', 'Optional']}>
  <Tab value="Required">
    **Required Information**

    These fields must be completed before you can publish:

    | Field | Description | Tips |
    |-------|-------------|------|
    | Display Name | Your professional name | Include credentials (e.g., "Dr. Maria Santos, PT, PhD") |
    | Profile Photo | Professional headshot | High-quality, friendly, professional attire |
    | Bio | 100-1000 characters | Highlight your expertise and approach |
    | Specializations | Areas of expertise | Select all that apply |
    | Languages | Languages you speak | Important for international patients |

  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Optional">
    **Optional Information**

    Enhance your profile with:

    - Years of experience
    - Education and certifications
    - Professional associations
    - Location (city/country)
    - Social media links (LinkedIn, website)

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Writing Your Bio

Your bio is often the first thing patients read. Make it count.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Opening

    Lead with your specialty and years of experience.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Approach

    Describe how you work with patients and your care philosophy.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Background

    Highlight key credentials, training, and certifications.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Personal Touch

    Share why you're passionate about this work.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Example Bio

> "I'm a pelvic floor physiotherapist with 12 years of experience helping women regain confidence and quality of life. I specialize in pregnancy preparation, postpartum recovery, and treating pelvic organ prolapse.
>
> My approach combines evidence-based techniques with compassionate care, ensuring each patient feels heard and supported throughout their journey.
>
> I hold a PhD in Women's Health Physiotherapy from the University of Lisbon and am certified in biofeedback and manual therapy techniques."

<Callout type="info">
  Write in first person ("I specialize in...") to create a personal connection with potential patients.
</Callout>

## Profile Photo Guidelines

<Tabs items={['Recommended', 'Avoid']}>
  <Tab value="Recommended">
    **Best Practices**

    - Use a recent, high-resolution photo
    - Dress professionally (as you would for a consultation)
    - Choose a neutral or professional background
    - Smile naturally — appear approachable
    - Ensure good lighting (natural light works best)

  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Avoid">
    **What Not to Do**

    - Group photos or photos with others
    - Heavily filtered or edited photos
    - Casual vacation or social photos
    - Logos or graphics instead of your photo

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Credentials & Verification

### Required Documents

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Identity Verification

    Upload a government-issued ID.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Professional License

    Provide your current, valid license in your field.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Insurance Proof

    Submit proof of professional liability coverage.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Review Process

    Our team reviews within 2-3 business days and sends confirmation when approved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Publishing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Review Information

    Go to **Expert Dashboard** → **Profile** and review all sections.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Publish

    Click **Publish Profile** to make your profile visible to patients.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Manage Visibility

    Toggle **Profile Visibility** in settings anytime. Existing appointments are not affected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<Tabs items={['Stand Out', 'Stay Current']}>
  <Tab value="Stand Out">
    **Differentiate Yourself**

    - Use a unique, memorable bio
    - Highlight specific training or certifications
    - Mention languages spoken
    - Describe your approach to patient care

  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Stay Current">
    **Keep It Updated**

    - Review your profile quarterly
    - Update credentials when renewed
    - Add new specializations as you gain expertise
    - Refresh your photo every 2-3 years

  </Tab>
