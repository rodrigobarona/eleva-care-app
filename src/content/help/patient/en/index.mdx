# Welcome to Eleva Care

Eleva Care connects you with trusted, independent women's health experts. Whether you're seeking support during pregnancy, postpartum care, pelvic health guidance, or menopause management, we're here to help you find the right expert.

## How Eleva Care Works

<Cards>
  <Card title="Find an Expert" href="/help/patient/booking">
    Browse our network of licensed healthcare professionals specializing in women's health.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Book Your Appointment" href="/help/patient/booking#scheduling">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Meet Your Expert" href="/help/patient/booking#consultation">

## Areas of Care

<Tabs items={['Pregnancy', 'Pelvic Health', 'Menopause', 'Sexual Health']}>
  <Tab value="Pregnancy">
    **Pregnancy & Postpartum Support**

    From conception through the fourth trimester, our experts provide comprehensive care including birth preparation, breastfeeding support, and postpartum recovery guidance.

  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Pelvic Health">
    **Pelvic Floor Specialists**

    Expert care for incontinence, prolapse, pelvic pain, and rehabilitation. Our specialists help you regain strength and confidence.

  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Menopause">
    **Menopause Management**

    Navigate hormonal transitions with guidance from endocrinologists, nutritionists, and specialists who understand your changing needs.

  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Sexual Health">
    **Sexual Health & Wellness**

    Compassionate, evidence-based support from therapists, counselors, and medical specialists in a judgment-free environment.

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Your First Steps

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Create an Account

    Sign up with your email or social login to access the platform.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Complete Your Profile

    Help us understand your needs so we can recommend the right experts for you.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Browse Experts

    Find specialists in your area of concern, filtered by specialty, language, and availability.

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Book an Appointment

    Select a time that works for you, complete payment, and receive instant confirmation.

  </Step>
</Steps>

  **Need Help?** Our support team is available to assist you at support@eleva.care
