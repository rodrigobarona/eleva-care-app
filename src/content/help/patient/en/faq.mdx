## Getting Started

<Accordions type="single">

<Accordion title="What is Eleva Care?" id="what-is-eleva-care">
Eleva Care is a digital health platform that connects patients with independent, licensed women's health experts. We provide the technology for booking, payments, and video consultations, while healthcare services are delivered by independent professionals.

Our platform specializes in:

- Pregnancy & postpartum care
- Pelvic health
- Menopause management
- Sexual health

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I create an account?" id="create-account">
  <Steps>
    <Step>### Visit eleva.care Go to [eleva.care](https://eleva.care) and click **Sign Up**</Step>
    <Step>### Choose your sign-up method Enter your email or use social login (Google, Apple)</Step>
    <Step>
      ### Complete your profile Add your basic information to help us personalize your experience
    </Step>
    <Step>### Start browsing Explore our network of women's health experts</Step>
  </Steps>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is Eleva Care free to use?" id="free-to-use">
**Yes, creating an account and browsing is completely free!**

You only pay when you book an appointment. Each expert sets their own consultation fees, which you'll see clearly before booking.

</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## Booking Appointments

<Accordions type="single">

<Accordion title="How do I find the right expert?" id="find-expert">
Use our powerful search and filter options:

| Filter                | Options                                            |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Specialty**         | Pregnancy, pelvic health, menopause, sexual health |
| **Language**          | English, Portuguese, Spanish, and more             |
| **Availability**      | Today, this week, specific dates                   |
| **Consultation type** | Video call, in-person                              |

<Callout type="idea">
  **Pro tip:** Read expert profiles and patient reviews to find the best match for your needs.
</Callout>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I book same-day appointments?" id="same-day">
It depends on the expert's availability. Some experts offer same-day appointments, while others may require advance booking.

**How to find same-day availability:**

1. Use the date filter and select "Today"
2. Browse experts with open slots
3. Book directly from their calendar

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I need to reschedule?" id="reschedule">
You can easily reschedule from your patient dashboard:

<Steps>
  <Step>### Go to My Appointments Find the appointment you want to reschedule</Step>
  <Step>### Click Reschedule Select a new date and time from available slots</Step>
  <Step>### Confirm Your expert will be notified automatically</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warn">
  **Important:** Reschedule at least 24 hours in advance to avoid fees.
</Callout>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I book appointments for family members?" id="family-members">
  Currently, each patient needs their own account. For minors, a parent or guardian should:

- Create the account
- Book the appointment
- Attend the consultation with the minor
  </Accordion>

</Accordions>

<Accordion title="What if I have technical issues?" id="tech-issues">
Don't panic! Try these steps:

1. **Refresh your browser** - Often fixes most issues
2. **Check your internet** - Try moving closer to your router
3. **Switch browsers** - Try Chrome if you're using another browser
4. **Contact support** - Email support@eleva.care for immediate help

<Callout type="info">
  Your expert will wait for you. If the issue persists, they can reschedule at no extra cost.
</Callout>

## Payments & Refunds

<Accordions type="single">

<Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?" id="payment-methods">
We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

| Method                     | Availability                       |
| -------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Credit/Debit Cards**     | Visa, Mastercard, Amex (worldwide) |
| **Multibanco**             | Portugal only                      |
| **Bank Transfers**         | Select regions                     |
| **Apple Pay / Google Pay** | Where available                    |

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="When am I charged?" id="when-charged">
You're charged **when you book** your appointment. The payment is held securely until 24 hours after your consultation is completed, then released to your expert.

This protects you in case of cancellations or no-shows.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What's your refund policy?" id="refund-policy">
Our cancellation and refund policy:

| When you cancel                  | Refund      |
| -------------------------------- | ----------- |
| **48+ hours** before appointment | 100% refund |
| **24-48 hours** before           | 50% refund  |
| **Less than 24 hours**           | No refund   |

<Callout type="info">
  Some experts have their own cancellation policies. Always check before booking.
</Callout>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use insurance?" id="insurance">
Eleva Care doesn't process insurance directly, but you can:
1. Download detailed receipts from your dashboard
2. Submit them to your insurance for reimbursement
3. Coverage varies by insurance provider and plan

Contact your insurance provider to check if telehealth consultations are covered.

</Accordion>

</Accordions>
