# Set Up Your Workspace

This guide walks you through the complete setup process for your workspace on Eleva Care—whether you're a medical practice, wellness center, employer, or healthcare institution.

## Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Apply for Partnership

    Before you can set up your workspace, apply for partnership:

1. Visit [eleva.care/contact?partner=true](https://eleva.care/contact?partner=true)
2. Fill out the partnership application form
3. Provide your credentials and services
    4. Our team reviews your application (typically 5 business days)

<Callout type="info">
      **Approval Requirements**: We review your credentials, services offered, and alignment with
      our women's health mission.
</Callout>

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Create Your Workspace Profile

    Once approved, access the Partner Dashboard to set up your profile.

    **Basic Information:**
    - **Workspace Name**: Your practice or business name
    - **Description**: A compelling description (up to 500 characters)
    - **Logo**: High-resolution logo (400x400px, PNG or SVG)
    - **Cover Image**: Banner image (1200x400px)

    **Contact Information:**
    - Business email, phone, website, and address

    **Service Categories:**
- Women's Health & OB/GYN
- Mental Health & Therapy
- Nutrition & Dietetics
- Fitness & Physical Therapy
- Wellness Coaching

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Configure Settings

    Set up your booking and payment preferences.

    <Tabs items={['Booking Settings', 'Payment Settings']}>
      <Tab value="Booking Settings">
        - **Cancellation Policy**: Set terms (24h, 48h, etc.)
        - **Buffer Time**: Time between appointments
- **Booking Window**: How far in advance clients can book
        - **Minimum Notice**: Minimum time before an appointment

      </Tab>
      <Tab value="Payment Settings">
        - **Connect Stripe**: Link your Stripe account for receiving payments
        - **Currency**: Set your preferred currency (EUR, USD, etc.)
        - **Payout Schedule**: Choose weekly or monthly payouts

      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

<Callout type="warning">
  **Important**: You must connect a Stripe account before your experts can receive payouts.
</Callout>

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Set Your Marketing Fee

    As a workspace owner, you earn a marketing fee from each booking:

| Workspace Tier | Marketing Fee | Expert Minimum |
|----------------|---------------|----------------|
| Basic | 10-15% | 70% |
| Standard | 15-20% | 65% |
| Premium | 20-25% | 60% |

    **Example**: For a €100 booking with 15% workspace fee:
    - Eleva Platform: €8-12 (8-12%)
    - Your Workspace: €15 (15%)
    - Expert: €73-77 (73-77%)

[Learn more about revenue sharing →](/docs/workspace/pricing/revenue)

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Invite Your Team

Once your workspace is set up, invite your healthcare professionals:

1. Go to **Team Management** in your dashboard
2. Click **Invite Expert**
3. Enter the expert's email address
4. They'll receive an invitation to join your workspace

[Learn about team management →](/docs/workspace/team/experts)

  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Go Live

    Before publishing your workspace, verify:

    - Workspace profile is complete
    - Stripe account is connected
    - Marketing fee is configured
    - At least one expert has joined
    - Expert profiles are complete and published

Click **Publish Workspace** to make your workspace visible to patients!

  </Step>
</Steps>
