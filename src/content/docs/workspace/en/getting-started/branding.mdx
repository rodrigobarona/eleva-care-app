# Customize Your Branding

Make your Eleva Care presence match your brand identity. This guide covers all customization options available to workspaces.

## Standard Branding (All Plans)

### Logo & Identity

Every workspace can customize:

- **Primary Logo**: Displayed in your workspace profile and expert listings
- **Cover Image**: Banner image for your workspace page
- **Brand Colors**: Primary and secondary colors for your profile

### Profile Customization

- **Workspace Description**: Tell your story and highlight what makes you unique
- **Services Offered**: List the healthcare categories you specialize in
- **Team Bio**: Introduce your team of healthcare professionals
- **Social Links**: Connect your Instagram, LinkedIn, and website

## Advanced Branding (Professional Plan)

### Custom Booking Pages

With the Professional plan, you unlock:

- **Custom URL Slug**: `eleva.care/your-workspace-name`
- **Branded Booking Flow**: Your logo and colors throughout the booking process
- **Custom Confirmation Emails**: Include your branding in client communications

### Multi-Location Support

If your workspace has multiple locations:

- Create separate profiles for each location
- Share experts across locations
- Track analytics per location

## White-Label Options (Enterprise Plan)

Enterprise partners can fully white-label the Eleva Care experience:

### Custom Domain

- Use your own domain: `telehealth.your-workspace.com`
- SSL certificate included
- Automatic redirects configured

### Full Brand Experience

- Remove Eleva Care branding
- Custom email sender domain
- Branded video consultation interface
- Custom footer and legal links

### API Integration

- Embed booking widgets in your existing website
- Custom integrations with your systems
- Webhook notifications for real-time updates

## Branding Best Practices

### Logo Guidelines

- **Format**: PNG or SVG (vector preferred)
- **Size**: Minimum 400x400px
- **Background**: Transparent or white
- **Style**: Clean, professional, healthcare-appropriate

### Cover Image Guidelines

- **Size**: 1200x400px (3:1 aspect ratio)
- **Content**: Professional imagery related to your services
- **Text**: Avoid text in images (doesn't scale well)
- **Quality**: High resolution, not pixelated

### Color Selection

When choosing brand colors:

1. **Primary Color**: Used for buttons and highlights
2. **Secondary Color**: Used for accents and hover states
3. **Text Colors**: Ensure sufficient contrast for accessibility

<Callout type="tip">
  Use your existing brand colors for consistency. Patients who know your brand will feel more comfortable booking through Eleva Care.
</Callout>
