## How It Works

### For Your Organization

1. **Apply & Get Approved** - Submit your partnership application (5 business days)
2. **Setup Your Profile** - Create your organization profile with branding and services
3. **Onboard Your Experts** - Invite healthcare professionals to join your practice
4. **Launch & Grow** - Start receiving bookings and track performance

## Revenue Model

Our three-party revenue model ensures fair compensation for everyone:

| Party | Revenue Share | Purpose |
|-------|--------------|---------|
| **Patient** | Pays full amount | For healthcare services |
| **Expert** | 60-77% minimum | Service delivery |
| **Organization** | 10-25% | Patient acquisition, marketing |
| **Eleva** | 8-20% | Platform, technology, payments |

<Callout type="success">
  **Expert Protection**: Experts always receive at least 60% of the booking amount. Combined fees (platform + organization) never exceed 40%.
</Callout>

[Learn about revenue sharing →](/docs/organization/pricing/revenue)
