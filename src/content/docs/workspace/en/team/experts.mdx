# Manage Your Experts

Your team of healthcare professionals is the heart of your organization. This guide covers everything you need to know about managing experts on Eleva Care.

## Expert Types

### In-House Experts

Healthcare professionals employed by or contracted with your organization:

- Work exclusively or primarily through your organization
- Use your organization's branding
- Subject to your marketing fee
- Managed through your dashboard

### Network Experts

Independent professionals from Eleva Care's expert network:

- Work with multiple organizations
- Maintain their individual brand
- Subject to your marketing fee when booked through your organization
- Can be featured on your organization profile

## Adding Experts to Your Organization

### Option 1: Invite Existing Eleva Care Experts

If the expert already has an Eleva Care profile:

1. Go to **Team Management** → **Invite Expert**
2. Enter their email address
3. They receive an invitation to join your organization
4. Once accepted, their profile appears under your organization

### Option 2: Invite New Experts

For experts new to Eleva Care:

1. Go to **Team Management** → **Invite New Expert**
2. Enter their email and name
3. They receive an invitation to create an Eleva Care profile
4. After setup, they're automatically added to your organization

<Callout type="info">
  **Expert Limits**: Starter plans support up to 3 experts, Professional up to 10, and Enterprise is unlimited.
</Callout>

## Expert Dashboard

From your organization dashboard, you can view:

### Expert Overview

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Total Experts** | Number of experts in your organization |
| **Active Experts** | Experts who have taken bookings in the last 30 days |
| **Published Profiles** | Experts with visible profiles |
| **Pending Invitations** | Invitations not yet accepted |

### Per-Expert Metrics

- **Bookings**: Total appointments scheduled
- **Revenue**: Gross revenue generated
- **Rating**: Average patient rating
- **Availability**: Scheduled hours available

## Expert Tier Benefits

Experts maintain their individual tier when joining your organization:

### Community Experts

- Platform fee: 12-20%
- Your organization fee: 10-25%
- Expert net: 60-77%

### Top Experts

- Platform fee: 8-15%
- Your organization fee: 10-25%
- Expert net: 60-82%

<Callout type="success">
  **Expert Protection**: Experts keep their tier benefits when joining an organization. Top experts still pay lower platform fees.
</Callout>

## Commission Visibility

Your experts can see a detailed breakdown of each booking:

```
Booking Amount: $100.00
├─ Eleva Platform Fee: $8.00 (8%)
├─ Organization Marketing Fee: $15.00 (15%)
└─ Expert Net: $77.00 (77%)
```

This transparency helps build trust with your team.

## Managing Expert Permissions

### Organization Admins Can:

- View expert availability and bookings
- Access aggregate analytics
- Configure organization-wide settings
- Remove experts from the organization

### Organization Admins Cannot:

- Change expert's individual settings
- Access expert's personal financial details
- Modify expert's profile without permission
- View bookings from other organizations

## Removing Experts

To remove an expert from your organization:

1. Go to **Team Management**
2. Find the expert
3. Click **Remove from Organization**
4. Confirm the action

<Callout type="warning">
  **Note**: Removing an expert doesn't delete their Eleva Care profile. They can continue working independently or with other organizations.
</Callout>

## Best Practices

### Onboarding

1. **Clear Expectations**: Explain your marketing fee and value proposition
2. **Support**: Help with profile optimization
3. **Training**: Introduce them to the platform features
4. **Communication**: Set up regular check-ins

### Retention

1. **Competitive Fees**: Keep your marketing fee reasonable
2. **Patient Volume**: Focus on driving bookings to your experts
3. **Support**: Provide administrative and marketing support
4. **Recognition**: Highlight top performers
