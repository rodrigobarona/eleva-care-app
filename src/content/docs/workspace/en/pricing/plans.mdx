## Starter Plan — €99/month

**Perfect for**: Small practices and wellness centers getting started with digital health services.

### Included Features

- Up to 3 expert profiles
- Basic analytics and reporting
- Scheduling and calendar management
- Video consultation platform
- Payment processing
- Email support
- Practice branding
- Client management tools

### Best For

- Solo practitioners expanding to a small team
- Wellness coaches forming a group practice
- Specialty practices testing digital services
- New organizations building their team

## Professional Plan — €199/month

**Perfect for**: Growing practices and established organizations looking to scale.

### Included Features

Everything in Starter, plus:

- Up to 10 expert profiles
- Advanced analytics and insights
- Customizable booking pages
- Priority support (24-hour response)
- Marketing support and resources
- Team management dashboard
- Multi-location support
- Custom domain integration (`eleva.care/your-organization`)

### Best For

- Growing multi-provider practices
- Established wellness centers
- Organizations with multiple locations
- Practices focused on scaling

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can change plans at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

### What happens if I exceed my expert limit?

You'll receive a notification when approaching your limit. To add more experts, you'll need to upgrade to the next plan tier.

### Is there a free trial?

We offer a 14-day free trial of the Professional plan for qualified organizations. Contact our sales team to get started.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

### Are there setup fees?

No setup fees. You pay only the monthly subscription and the platform fees on bookings.
