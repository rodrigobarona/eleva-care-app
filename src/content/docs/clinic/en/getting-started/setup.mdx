# Set Up Your Organization

This guide walks you through the complete setup process for your clinic or healthcare organization on Eleva Care.

## Step 1: Apply for Partnership

Before you can set up your organization, you need to apply for partnership:

1. Visit [eleva.care/contact?partner=true](https://eleva.care/contact?partner=true)
2. Fill out the partnership application form
3. Provide your organization credentials and services
4. Our team will review your application (typically 5 business days)

<Callout type="info">
  **Approval Requirements**: We review your practice credentials, services offered, and alignment with our women's health mission.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Create Organization Profile

Once approved, you'll have access to the Partner Dashboard:

### Basic Information

- **Organization Name**: Your clinic or practice name
- **Description**: A compelling description of your services (up to 500 characters)
- **Logo**: High-resolution logo (recommended: 400x400px, PNG or SVG)
- **Cover Image**: Banner image for your profile (recommended: 1200x400px)

### Contact Information

- **Business Email**: Primary contact for partnership communications
- **Phone Number**: Business phone (optional)
- **Website**: Your organization's website URL
- **Address**: Physical location (if applicable)

### Service Categories

Select the healthcare categories your organization covers:

- Women's Health & OB/GYN
- Mental Health & Therapy
- Nutrition & Dietetics
- Fitness & Physical Therapy
- Wellness Coaching
- Other specialties

## Step 3: Configure Settings

### Booking Settings

- **Cancellation Policy**: Set your cancellation terms (24h, 48h, etc.)
- **Buffer Time**: Time between appointments (15, 30, 60 minutes)
- **Booking Window**: How far in advance clients can book
- **Minimum Notice**: Minimum time before an appointment can be booked

### Payment Settings

1. **Connect Stripe**: Link your Stripe account for receiving payments
2. **Currency**: Set your preferred currency (EUR, USD, etc.)
3. **Payout Schedule**: Choose weekly or monthly payouts

<Callout type="warning">
  **Important**: You must connect a Stripe account before your experts can receive payouts.
</Callout>

## Step 4: Set Your Marketing Fee

As a clinic, you earn a marketing fee from each booking made through your experts:

| Clinic Tier | Marketing Fee | Expert Minimum |
|-------------|---------------|----------------|
| Basic | 10-15% | 70% |
| Standard | 15-20% | 65% |
| Premium | 20-25% | 60% |

**Example**: For a $100 booking with 15% clinic fee:
- Eleva Platform: $8-12 (8-12%)
- Your Clinic: $15 (15%)
- Expert: $73-77 (73-77%)

[Learn more about revenue sharing →](/docs/clinic/pricing/revenue)

## Step 5: Invite Your Team

Once your organization is set up, invite your healthcare professionals:

1. Go to **Team Management** in your dashboard
2. Click **Invite Expert**
3. Enter the expert's email address
4. They'll receive an invitation to join your organization

[Learn about team management →](/docs/clinic/team/experts)

## Step 6: Go Live

Before publishing your organization:

- [ ] Organization profile is complete
- [ ] Stripe account is connected
- [ ] Marketing fee is configured
- [ ] At least one expert has joined
- [ ] Expert profiles are complete and published

Click **Publish Organization** to make your clinic visible to patients!
