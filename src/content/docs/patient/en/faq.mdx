# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using Eleva Care.

## Getting Started

### What is Eleva Care?

Eleva Care is a digital health platform that connects patients with independent, licensed women's health experts. We provide the technology for booking, payments, and video consultations, while healthcare services are delivered by independent professionals.

### How do I create an account?

1. Visit [eleva.care](https://eleva.care)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email or use social login
4. Complete your profile
5. Start browsing experts

### Is Eleva Care free to use?

Creating an account and browsing experts is free. You only pay when you book an appointment. Each expert sets their own consultation fees.

## Booking Appointments

### How do I find the right expert?

Use our search and filter options:
- Browse by specialty (pregnancy, pelvic health, menopause, etc.)
- Filter by language, location, and availability
- Read expert profiles and patient reviews
- View credentials and experience

### Can I book same-day appointments?

Availability depends on the expert. Some experts offer same-day appointments, while others may require advance booking. Check the expert's calendar for available times.

### What if I need to reschedule?

You can reschedule from your patient dashboard:
1. Go to **My Appointments**
2. Click on the appointment
3. Select **Reschedule**
4. Choose a new time

Reschedule at least 24 hours in advance to avoid fees.

### Can I book appointments for family members?

Currently, each patient needs their own account. For minors, a parent or guardian should create the account and attend the consultation.

## Consultations

### How do video consultations work?

1. Book your appointment
2. Receive confirmation with video link
3. Click **Join Call** at your appointment time
4. Connect with your expert via secure video

### What technology do I need?

- A device with camera and microphone (phone, tablet, or computer)
- Stable internet connection
- Updated web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge)

### What if I have technical issues during my call?

If you experience issues:
1. Refresh your browser
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact support@eleva.care for immediate help

### Are consultations confidential?

Yes. All consultations are private and encrypted. Only you and your expert can access your session. We comply with healthcare privacy regulations.

## Payments

### What payment methods do you accept?

- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Multibanco (Portugal)
- Bank transfers (select regions)

### When am I charged?

You're charged when you book your appointment. Payment is held securely until 24 hours after your consultation is completed.

### Can I get a refund?

Refund eligibility depends on when you cancel:
- 48+ hours before: Full refund
- 24-48 hours before: 50% refund
- Less than 24 hours: No refund

Some experts have their own cancellation policies, so check before booking.

### Can I use insurance?

Eleva Care doesn't process insurance directly, but you can download receipts for insurance reimbursement claims. Coverage varies by insurance provider.

## Account & Privacy

### How do I update my profile?

1. Click your profile icon
2. Go to **Account Settings**
3. Update your information
4. Save changes

### Is my health information secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices:
- Data encrypted in transit and at rest
- GDPR and LGPD compliant
- Regular security audits
- No selling of personal data

### How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:
1. Go to **Account Settings**
2. Click **Delete Account**
3. Confirm deletion

Note: You must complete or cancel any pending appointments first.

## About Our Experts

### How are experts vetted?

All experts on Eleva Care:
- Are licensed healthcare professionals
- Undergo identity verification
- Have their credentials reviewed
- Agree to our professional standards

### What's the difference between Community and Top Experts?

**Community Experts** are verified professionals on our platform.

**Top Experts** have earned premium status through:
- High patient ratings (4.8+ stars)
- Consistent appointment completion
- Excellent response times
- Extended platform experience

### Can I contact an expert before booking?

Some experts allow pre-booking inquiries. Check the expert's profile for contact options.

## Technical Support

### What browsers are supported?

We support the latest versions of:
- Google Chrome
- Safari
- Mozilla Firefox
- Microsoft Edge

### Does Eleva Care have a mobile app?

Currently, Eleva Care is a web-based platform optimized for mobile browsers. A dedicated mobile app is coming soon.

Contact us at support@eleva.care with:
- Description of the issue
- Device and browser you're using
- Screenshots if possible
