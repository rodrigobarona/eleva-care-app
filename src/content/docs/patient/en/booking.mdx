# How to Book an Appointment

Booking an appointment on Eleva Care is simple and takes just a few minutes. Follow this guide to find the right expert and schedule your consultation.

## Finding an Expert

### Browse by Specialty

Our experts specialize in different areas of women's health:

- **Pregnancy & Birth Preparation** - Physiotherapists, OB-GYNs, midwives
- **Postpartum Recovery** - Pelvic floor specialists, mental health professionals
- **Pelvic Health** - Specialists in incontinence, prolapse, pelvic pain
- **Menopause & Hormonal Health** - Endocrinologists, nutritionists
- **Sexual Health** - Therapists, counselors, medical specialists

### Using Filters

Narrow down your search with filters:

- **Location** - Find experts in your region or who offer remote consultations
- **Language** - Filter by language spoken
- **Availability** - Show only experts with open appointments
- **Rating** - Sort by patient reviews

## Scheduling Your Appointment

### Step 1: Select a Time Slot

1. Click on the expert's profile
2. View their available time slots
3. Select a date and time that works for you

### Step 2: Confirm Your Details

1. Review the appointment type (video call or in-person)
2. Confirm the duration and price
3. Add any notes for the expert (optional)

### Step 3: Complete Payment

1. Choose your payment method
2. Enter payment details
3. Confirm your booking

<Callout type="success">
  You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details of your appointment.
</Callout>

## Managing Your Appointments

### View Upcoming Appointments

Access your appointments from your patient dashboard:

1. Go to **My Appointments**
2. View upcoming and past appointments
3. Access video call links when it's time

### Rescheduling

Need to change your appointment time?

1. Go to your appointment details
2. Click **Reschedule**
3. Select a new time slot
4. Confirm the change

<Callout type="warning">
  **Cancellation Policy**: Please reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment to avoid cancellation fees.
</Callout>

### Canceling an Appointment

If you need to cancel:

1. Go to your appointment details
2. Click **Cancel Appointment**
3. Confirm the cancellation

Refund policies vary by expert. Check the expert's cancellation policy before booking.

## Joining Your Consultation

### Video Consultations

For video appointments:

1. You'll receive a reminder email 24 hours before
2. Click the **Join Call** button 5 minutes before your appointment
3. Ensure your camera and microphone are working
4. Have a stable internet connection

### In-Person Appointments

For in-person visits:

1. Check the location details in your confirmation email
2. Arrive 10 minutes early
3. Bring any relevant medical records

## After Your Appointment

### Leave a Review

Help other patients by sharing your experience:

1. Go to **My Appointments** > **Past**
2. Click **Leave Review**
3. Rate your experience and add comments

### Book a Follow-Up

Many patients benefit from ongoing care:

1. Visit the expert's profile
2. Book another appointment
3. Reference your previous consultation in the notes
