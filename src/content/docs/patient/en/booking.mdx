# How to Book an Appointment

Booking an appointment on Eleva Care is simple and takes just a few minutes. Follow this guide to find the right expert and schedule your consultation.

## Finding an Expert

### Browse by Specialty

  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Postpartum">

    Pelvic floor specialists and mental health professionals supporting your recovery journey.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Pelvic Health">

    Specialists in incontinence, prolapse, and pelvic pain management.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Menopause">

    Endocrinologists and nutritionists guiding you through hormonal transitions.
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Sexual Health">
    **Sexual Health**

    Therapists, counselors, and medical specialists providing compassionate care.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Refine Your Search

Use filters to find your ideal expert:

| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Location | Find experts in your region or offering remote consultations |
| Language | Filter by languages spoken |
| Availability | Show only experts with open appointments |
| Rating | Sort by patient reviews |

## Booking Process

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Select a Time Slot

    Click on the expert's profile, view their available time slots, and select a date and time that works for you.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Confirm Your Details

    Review the appointment type (video call or in-person), confirm the duration and price, and add any notes for the expert.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Complete Payment

    Choose your payment method, enter payment details, and confirm your booking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success">
  You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details of your appointment, including the video call link if applicable.
</Callout>

## Managing Appointments

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="View Upcoming Appointments">
    Access your appointments from your patient dashboard:

    1. Go to **My Appointments**
    2. View upcoming and past appointments
    3. Access video call links when it's time
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Rescheduling">
    Need to change your appointment time?

    1. Go to your appointment details
    2. Click **Reschedule**
    3. Select a new time slot
    4. Confirm the change

    <Callout type="warning">
      Please reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment to avoid cancellation fees.
    </Callout>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Canceling an Appointment">
    If you need to cancel:

    1. Go to your appointment details
    2. Click **Cancel Appointment**
    3. Confirm the cancellation

    Refund policies vary by expert. Check the expert's cancellation policy before booking.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Joining Your Consultation

<Tabs items={['Video Call', 'In-Person']}>
  <Tab value="Video Call">
    **Video Consultations**

    <Steps>
      <Step>
        ### Preparation

        You'll receive a reminder email 24 hours before your appointment with a link to join.
      </Step>
      <Step>
        ### Join the Call

        Click the **Join Call** button 5 minutes before your appointment time.
      </Step>
      <Step>
        ### Technical Setup

        Ensure your camera and microphone are working and you have a stable internet connection.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="In-Person">
    **In-Person Appointments**

    <Steps>
      <Step>
        ### Check Location

        Review the location details in your confirmation email.
      </Step>
      <Step>
        ### Arrive Early

        Plan to arrive 10 minutes before your appointment time.
      </Step>
      <Step>
        ### Bring Documents

        Bring any relevant medical records or test results.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
