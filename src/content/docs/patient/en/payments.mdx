# Payments & Pricing

Eleva Care provides a secure, transparent payment system. This guide covers everything you need to know about paying for your appointments.

## Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

### Credit & Debit Cards

- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express

### Local Payment Methods

Depending on your region:

- **Portugal**: Multibanco (bank transfer)
- **Spain**: Bank transfers
- **Brazil**: PIX (coming soon)

<Callout type="info">
  All payments are processed securely through Stripe, a leading payment provider trusted by millions of businesses worldwide.
</Callout>

## How Pricing Works

### Expert-Set Pricing

Each expert sets their own consultation fees. Prices vary based on:

- **Expertise level** - Community vs. Top Experts
- **Consultation type** - Initial vs. follow-up
- **Duration** - 30, 45, or 60-minute sessions
- **Specialty** - Different areas of expertise

### What's Included

Your payment covers:

- ✅ The full consultation time
- ✅ Secure video call platform
- ✅ Session notes (if shared by expert)
- ✅ Follow-up recommendations

### What's NOT Included

- ❌ Prescription medications
- ❌ Lab tests or diagnostic procedures
- ❌ In-person examinations (unless specified)

## Payment Process

### When You Pay

Payment is collected when you book your appointment:

1. Select your appointment time
2. Enter payment details
3. Payment is authorized
4. Confirmation email sent

### Payment Hold

Your payment is held securely until 24 hours after your appointment is completed. This protects both you and the expert.

## Refunds & Cancellations

### Cancellation Windows

| Timeframe | Refund Amount |
|-----------|---------------|
| 48+ hours before | 100% refund |
| 24-48 hours before | 50% refund |
| Less than 24 hours | No refund |

<Callout type="warning">
  Cancellation policies may vary by expert. Check the expert's profile for their specific policy.
</Callout>

### How to Request a Refund

1. Go to **My Appointments**
2. Find the appointment
3. Click **Cancel** or **Request Refund**
4. Follow the prompts

Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days to your original payment method.

### Disputed Charges

If you have an issue with a charge:

1. Contact the expert first to resolve the issue
2. If unresolved, contact support@eleva.care
3. We'll investigate and mediate

## Receipts & Invoices

### Accessing Your Receipts

1. Go to **Patient Portal** > **Billing**
2. View all past payments
3. Download PDF receipts

### Invoice Details

Each receipt includes:

- Date and time of service
- Expert name and credentials
- Service description
- Payment amount
- Transaction ID

### For Insurance Reimbursement

Some insurance plans may reimburse telehealth consultations. Your receipt includes all necessary details for submitting a claim.

## Security

### Data Protection

- All payment data is encrypted
- We never store your full card number
- PCI DSS compliant

### Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA for additional account security in your account settings.

## FAQ

### Can I save my payment method?

Yes, you can save your card for faster checkout. Your card details are stored securely by Stripe.

### Are there any hidden fees?

No. The price shown when booking is the total amount you'll pay. No platform fees or hidden charges.

### What currency will I be charged in?

You'll be charged in the currency shown at checkout, typically based on the expert's location.
