# Payments & Pricing

Eleva Care provides a secure, transparent payment system. This guide covers everything you need to know about paying for your appointments.

## Accepted Payment Methods

<Tabs items={['Cards', 'Regional Methods']}>
  <Tab value="Cards">
    **Credit & Debit Cards**

    We accept all major cards processed securely through Stripe:

    - Visa
    - Mastercard
    - American Express
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Regional Methods">
    **Local Payment Options**

    Depending on your region, additional methods may be available:

    | Region | Method | Status |
    |--------|--------|--------|
    | Portugal | Multibanco | Available |
    | Spain | Bank Transfer | Available |
    | Brazil | PIX | Coming Soon |
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout type="info">
  All payments are processed securely through Stripe, a leading payment provider trusted by millions of businesses worldwide.
</Callout>

## How Pricing Works

### Expert-Set Pricing

Each expert sets their own consultation fees based on:

| Factor | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Expertise Level | Community vs. Top Experts |
| Consultation Type | Initial vs. follow-up |
| Duration | 30, 45, or 60-minute sessions |
| Specialty | Different areas of expertise |

### What's Included

<Tabs items={['Included', 'Not Included']}>
  <Tab value="Included">
    Your payment covers:

    - Full consultation time as booked
    - Access to secure video call platform
    - Session notes (if shared by expert)
    - Follow-up recommendations
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Not Included">
    Your payment does not cover:

    - Prescription medications
    - Lab tests or diagnostic procedures
    - In-person examinations (unless specified)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Refunds & Cancellations

### Cancellation Windows

| Timeframe | Refund Amount |
|-----------|---------------|
| 48+ hours before | 100% refund |
| 24-48 hours before | 50% refund |
| Less than 24 hours | No refund |

<Callout type="warning">
  Cancellation policies may vary by expert. Always check the expert's profile for their specific policy before booking.
</Callout>

### How to Request a Refund

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Access Your Appointments

    Go to **My Appointments** in your patient portal.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Find the Appointment

    Locate the appointment you wish to cancel.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Request Refund

    Click **Cancel** or **Request Refund** and follow the prompts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days to your original payment method.

### Disputed Charges

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="How to resolve a disputed charge">
    If you have an issue with a charge:

    1. **Contact the expert first** - Many issues can be resolved directly
    2. **Contact support** - Email support@eleva.care if unresolved
    3. **Investigation** - We'll investigate and mediate the dispute
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Can I save my payment method?">
    Yes, you can save your card for faster checkout. Your card details are stored securely by Stripe, not on our servers.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are there any hidden fees?">
    No. The price shown when booking is the total amount you'll pay. No platform fees or hidden charges.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What currency will I be charged in?">
    You'll be charged in the currency shown at checkout, typically based on the expert's location.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
