# Reviews & Ratings

Your feedback helps other patients find the right expert and helps experts improve their services. Learn how our review system works.

## Leaving a Review

You can leave a review after your appointment is completed. You'll receive an email reminder within 24 hours.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Access Past Appointments

    Go to **My Appointments** â†’ **Past** in your patient portal.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Select the Appointment

    Find the completed appointment you want to review.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Rate Your Experience

    Click **Leave Review** and rate from 1-5 stars.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Add Feedback

    Write about your experience (optional but encouraged).
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Review Guidelines

<Tabs items={['Recommended', 'Avoid']}>
  <Tab value="Recommended">
    **Best Practices**

    - Be specific about your experience
    - Mention what was particularly helpful
    - Share constructive feedback
    - Keep comments professional
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Avoid">
    **Please Avoid**

    - Including personal medical details
    - Using offensive language
    - Sharing the expert's private information
    - Reviewing appointments you didn't attend
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout type="info">
  Reviews are moderated to ensure they follow our community guidelines. Most reviews are published within 24 hours.
</Callout>

## Understanding Ratings

### Star Rating System

| Rating | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 5 Stars | Excellent - Exceeded expectations |
| 4 Stars | Very Good - Met expectations |
| 3 Stars | Good - Room for improvement |
| 2 Stars | Fair - Some issues |
| 1 Star | Poor - Significant problems |

### Rating Criteria

When leaving a review, consider these aspects:

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Communication">
    Was the expert clear and easy to understand? Did they explain things in a way you could follow?
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Professionalism">
    Was the consultation handled professionally? Did you feel respected and heard?
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Expertise">
    Did the expert demonstrate knowledge in their field? Were they able to answer your questions?
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Helpfulness">
    Did you receive useful guidance? Do you have a clear path forward?
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Punctuality">
    Did the appointment start on time? Was your time respected?
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

### Expert Badges

Experts may earn recognition badges based on performance:

| Badge | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| Top Expert | Consistently high ratings (4.8+ average) |
| Quick Responder | Responds to inquiries within 24 hours |
| Highly Recommended | High percentage of repeat bookings |

## Reading Reviews

### Finding Reviews

View expert reviews directly on their profile page:

1. Navigate to the expert's profile
2. Scroll to the **Reviews** section
3. Read patient feedback
4. Filter by rating or date

<Callout>
  All reviews on Eleva Care are from verified patients who completed an appointment. This ensures authentic, trustworthy feedback.
</Callout>

### Review Statistics

Each expert profile displays:

- Overall star rating
- Total number of reviews
- Rating breakdown by category
- Recent review highlights

## Managing Your Reviews

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Editing Your Review">
    Made a mistake? You can edit your review within 7 days:

    1. Go to **My Reviews** in your patient portal
    2. Find the review you want to edit
    3. Click **Edit** and make your changes
    4. Save your updates
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Deleting Your Review">
    To remove your review:

    1. Go to **My Reviews**
    2. Click **Delete** on the review
    3. Confirm deletion

    <Callout type="warning">
      Once deleted, your review cannot be recovered. You can leave a new review if desired.
    </Callout>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Expert Responses">
    Experts may respond to reviews. If an expert responds:

    - You'll receive a notification
    - View the response on the review page
    - Responses are visible to all users
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Reporting Concerns

### Inappropriate Reviews

If you see a review that violates our guidelines:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Report the Review

    Click **Report** on the review in question.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Select a Reason

    Choose the most appropriate reason for reporting.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Submit

    Our team will investigate and take action if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Serious Concerns

For significant issues with an expert, contact support@eleva.care directly with details of your concern. Our team will investigate promptly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Are reviews anonymous?">
    Reviews show your first name and last initial (e.g., "Maria S."). Your full name is not displayed publicly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can experts see who left negative reviews?">
    Experts can see the first name and initial of all reviewers, regardless of rating.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long do reviews stay visible?">
    Reviews remain visible indefinitely unless you delete them or they violate our guidelines.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I review if the expert canceled?">
    No, reviews can only be left for completed appointments.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
