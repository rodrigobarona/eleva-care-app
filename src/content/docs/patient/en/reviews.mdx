# Reviews & Ratings

Your feedback helps other patients find the right expert and helps experts improve their services. Learn how our review system works.

## Leaving a Review

### When Can You Review?

You can leave a review after your appointment is completed. You'll receive an email reminder within 24 hours.

### How to Leave a Review

1. Go to **My Appointments** > **Past**
2. Find the completed appointment
3. Click **Leave Review**
4. Rate your experience (1-5 stars)
5. Add written feedback (optional but encouraged)
6. Submit your review

### Review Guidelines

To ensure helpful reviews:

**Do:**

- Be specific about your experience
- Mention what was helpful
- Share constructive feedback
- Keep it professional

**Don't:**

- Include personal medical details
- Use offensive language
- Share the expert's private information
- Review appointments you didn't attend

<Callout type="info">
  Reviews are moderated to ensure they follow our community guidelines. Most reviews are published
  within 24 hours.
</Callout>

## Understanding Ratings

### Star Ratings

Our 5-star rating system:

| Stars          | Meaning                           |
| -------------- | --------------------------------- |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (5) | Excellent - Exceeded expectations |
| ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (4)   | Very Good - Met expectations      |
| ⭐⭐⭐ (3)     | Good - Room for improvement       |
| ⭐⭐ (2)       | Fair - Some issues                |
| ⭐ (1)         | Poor - Significant problems       |

### What We Rate

When leaving a review, consider:

- **Communication** - Was the expert clear and easy to understand?
- **Professionalism** - Was the consultation handled professionally?
- **Expertise** - Did the expert demonstrate knowledge in their field?
- **Helpfulness** - Did you receive useful guidance?
- **Punctuality** - Did the appointment start on time?

### Expert Badges

Experts may earn badges based on performance:

- **Top Expert** - Consistently high ratings (4.8+ average)
- **Quick Responder** - Responds to inquiries within 24 hours
- **Highly Recommended** - High percentage of repeat bookings

## Reading Reviews

### Finding Reviews

View expert reviews on their profile page:

1. Go to the expert's profile
2. Scroll to the **Reviews** section
3. Read patient feedback
4. Filter by rating or date

### Verified Reviews

All reviews on Eleva Care are from verified patients who completed an appointment. This ensures authentic feedback.

### Review Statistics

Each expert profile shows:

- Overall star rating
- Total number of reviews
- Rating breakdown by category
- Recent review highlights

## Editing or Removing Reviews

### Editing Your Review

Made a mistake? You can edit your review within 7 days:

1. Go to **My Reviews** in your patient portal
2. Find the review
3. Click **Edit**
4. Make your changes
5. Save

### Requesting Removal

To remove your review:

1. Go to **My Reviews**
2. Click **Delete** on the review
3. Confirm deletion

<Callout type="warning">
  Once deleted, your review cannot be recovered. You can leave a new review if desired.
</Callout>

## Responding to Expert Responses

Experts may respond to reviews. If an expert responds to your review:

1. You'll receive a notification
2. View the response on the review page
3. You cannot reply to responses

## Reporting Concerns

### Inappropriate Reviews

If you see a review that violates our guidelines:

1. Click **Report** on the review
2. Select the reason
3. Submit your report

We'll investigate and take action if needed.

### Dispute Resolution

If you have a serious concern about an expert:

1. Contact support@eleva.care
2. Provide details of the issue
3. Our team will investigate

## FAQ

### Are reviews anonymous?

Reviews show your first name and last initial (e.g., "Maria S."). Your full name is not displayed.

### Can experts see who left negative reviews?

Experts can see the first name and initial of all reviewers, regardless of rating.

### How long do reviews stay visible?

Reviews remain visible indefinitely unless you delete them or they violate our guidelines.

### Can I review if the expert canceled?

No, reviews can only be left for completed appointments.
