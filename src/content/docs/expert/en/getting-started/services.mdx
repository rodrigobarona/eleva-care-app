# Creating Services

Services (also called "events" in the system) are the consultations you offer to patients. Each service has its own description, duration, and price.

## Service Components

### Required Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Title** | Clear, descriptive name (e.g., "Initial Pelvic Health Consultation") |
| **Description** | What patients can expect from this service |
| **Duration** | Consultation length (30, 45, 60, or 90 minutes) |
| **Price** | Your fee for this service |
| **Category** | Primary service category |

### Optional Fields

- Preparation instructions for patients
- Follow-up information
- Required materials or forms
- Video vs. in-person availability

## Types of Services

### Initial Consultations

First-time appointments for new patients:

- Longer duration (typically 60-90 minutes)
- Comprehensive assessment
- Treatment planning
- Higher price point

**Example:**
> **Initial Pelvic Floor Assessment**
> 60 minutes | €120
>
> Comprehensive evaluation including health history, symptom assessment, and personalized treatment recommendations.

### Follow-Up Sessions

Ongoing appointments for established patients:

- Shorter duration (30-45 minutes)
- Progress review
- Treatment continuation
- Lower price point

**Example:**
> **Follow-Up Pelvic Floor Session**
> 45 minutes | €80
>
> Continue your treatment plan with exercises, progress assessment, and adjustments as needed.

### Specialized Services

Targeted services for specific needs:

**Examples:**
- Pregnancy preparation session
- Postpartum recovery assessment
- Menopause symptom consultation
- Pre-surgical preparation

## Creating Your First Service

### Step 1: Navigate to Services

1. Go to **Expert Dashboard**
2. Click **Services** in the menu
3. Click **Create Service**

### Step 2: Basic Information

1. Enter a clear, descriptive title
2. Select the primary category
3. Choose the service duration
4. Set your price

### Step 3: Write Description

Your description should answer:

- What happens during this consultation?
- Who is this service for?
- What outcomes can patients expect?
- What should patients prepare beforehand?

<Callout type="tip">
  **Pro Tip**: Use bullet points to make your description scannable. Patients often skim before booking.
</Callout>

### Step 4: Configure Options

- **Video Consultation**: Enable for remote appointments
- **In-Person**: Enable if you see patients in a physical location
- **Buffer Time**: Add time before/after for notes
- **Advance Notice**: Minimum booking lead time

### Step 5: Publish

Click **Save & Publish** to make your service available for booking.

## Pricing Your Services

### Factors to Consider

- Your expertise level and credentials
- Local market rates
- Session duration
- Consultation complexity
- Your platform fees (8-20% depending on plan)

### Pricing Guidelines

| Service Type | Typical Range |
|--------------|---------------|
| Initial Consultation (60 min) | €80 - €150 |
| Follow-Up (45 min) | €60 - €100 |
| Specialized Session (60 min) | €100 - €180 |
| Quick Check-In (30 min) | €40 - €70 |

<Callout type="info">
  These are guidelines only. Your pricing should reflect your expertise, credentials, and target market.
</Callout>

### Platform Fees

Remember to factor in platform fees when pricing:

| Plan | Commission |
|------|------------|
| Commission-Only (Community) | 20% |
| Subscription (Community) | 12% |
| Commission-Only (Top Expert) | 15% |
| Subscription (Top Expert) | 8% |

**Example**: If you charge €100 and are on the 12% subscription plan, you receive €88.

## Service Limits

### Community Experts

- Up to 5 active services
- Basic customization options

### Top Experts

- Unlimited services
- Advanced customization
- Package offerings (coming soon)

## Managing Services

### Editing Services

1. Go to **Services**
2. Click on the service
3. Click **Edit**
4. Make changes
5. Click **Save**

<Callout type="warning">
  Changes to existing services don't affect already-booked appointments.
</Callout>

### Deactivating Services

To temporarily hide a service:

1. Go to the service
2. Toggle **Active** off
3. Service won't appear for new bookings

### Deleting Services

To permanently remove a service:

1. Ensure no pending appointments
2. Click **Delete Service**
3. Confirm deletion

## Best Practices

### Clear Naming

**Good:**
- "Initial Pelvic Floor Consultation"
- "Postpartum Recovery Assessment"
- "Menopause Symptom Management"

**Avoid:**
- "Consultation 1"
- "Session"
- "Meeting"

### Detailed Descriptions

Include:
- What you'll cover
- Who should book this
- What to expect
- Preparation needed

### Competitive Pricing

- Research similar experts
- Consider your experience level
- Start moderate, adjust based on demand
