# Creating Services

Services are the consultations you offer to patients. Each service has its own description, duration, and price.

## Service Components

<Tabs items={['Required', 'Optional']}>
  <Tab value="Required">
    **Required Fields**

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
    | Title | Clear, descriptive name (e.g., "Initial Pelvic Health Consultation") |
    | Description | What patients can expect from this service |
    | Duration | Consultation length (30, 45, 60, or 90 minutes) |
    | Price | Your fee for this service |
    | Category | Primary service category |
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Optional">
    **Optional Fields**

- Preparation instructions for patients
- Follow-up information
- Required materials or forms
- Video vs. in-person availability
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Types of Services

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Initial Consultations">
First-time appointments for new patients:

- Longer duration (typically 60-90 minutes)
- Comprehensive assessment
- Treatment planning
- Higher price point

**Example:**
> **Initial Pelvic Floor Assessment**
> 60 minutes | €120
>
> Comprehensive evaluation including health history, symptom assessment, and personalized treatment recommendations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Follow-Up Sessions">
Ongoing appointments for established patients:

- Shorter duration (30-45 minutes)
- Progress review
- Treatment continuation
- Lower price point

**Example:**
> **Follow-Up Pelvic Floor Session**
> 45 minutes | €80
>
> Continue your treatment plan with exercises, progress assessment, and adjustments as needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Specialized Services">
Targeted services for specific needs:

- Pregnancy preparation session
- Postpartum recovery assessment
- Menopause symptom consultation
- Pre-surgical preparation
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Creating Your First Service

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Navigate to Services

    Go to **Expert Dashboard** → **Services** → **Create Service**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Basic Information

    Enter a clear, descriptive title, select the primary category, choose duration, and set your price.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Write Description

    Answer: What happens during this consultation? Who is it for? What outcomes can patients expect? What should they prepare?
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Configure Options

    Enable video consultation, in-person options, buffer time, and advance notice requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Publish

    Click **Save & Publish** to make your service available for booking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  Use bullet points in your description to make it scannable. Patients often skim before booking.
</Callout>

## Pricing Your Services

### Factors to Consider

| Factor | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Expertise | Your credentials and experience level |
| Market | Local rates and competitor pricing |
| Duration | Session length and complexity |
| Platform Fees | 8-20% depending on your plan |

### Pricing Guidelines

| Service Type | Typical Range |
|--------------|---------------|
| Initial Consultation (60 min) | €80 - €150 |
| Follow-Up (45 min) | €60 - €100 |
| Specialized Session (60 min) | €100 - €180 |
| Quick Check-In (30 min) | €40 - €70 |

### Platform Fees

| Plan | Commission |
|------|------------|
| Commission-Only (Community) | 20% |
| Subscription (Community) | 12% |
| Commission-Only (Top Expert) | 15% |
| Subscription (Top Expert) | 8% |

**Example**: If you charge €100 and are on the 12% subscription plan, you receive €88.

## Managing Services

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Editing Services">
1. Go to **Services**
2. Click on the service
3. Click **Edit**
    4. Make changes and **Save**

<Callout type="warning">
  Changes to existing services don't affect already-booked appointments.
</Callout>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Deactivating Services">
To temporarily hide a service:

1. Go to the service
2. Toggle **Active** off
3. Service won't appear for new bookings
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Deleting Services">
To permanently remove a service:

1. Ensure no pending appointments
2. Click **Delete Service**
3. Confirm deletion
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Best Practices

<Tabs items={['Naming', 'Descriptions', 'Pricing']}>
  <Tab value="Naming">
    **Clear Naming**

**Good:**
- "Initial Pelvic Floor Consultation"
- "Postpartum Recovery Assessment"
- "Menopause Symptom Management"

**Avoid:**
- "Consultation 1"
- "Session"
- "Meeting"
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Descriptions">
    **Detailed Descriptions**

Include:
- What you'll cover
- Who should book this
- What to expect
- Preparation needed
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Pricing">
    **Competitive Pricing**

- Research similar experts
- Consider your experience level
- Start moderate, adjust based on demand
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
