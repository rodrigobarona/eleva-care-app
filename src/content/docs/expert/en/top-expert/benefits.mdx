# Top Expert Benefits

Top Expert status unlocks premium features and benefits designed to help you grow your practice and maximize your earnings.

## Financial Benefits

<Tabs items={['Lower Fees', 'Faster Payouts']}>
  <Tab value="Lower Fees">
    **Lower Platform Fees**

    Keep more of what you earn:

    | Plan | Community | Top Expert | Savings |
    |------|-----------|------------|---------|
    | Commission-Only | 20% | 15% | 5% per booking |
    | Subscription | 12% | 8% | 4% per booking |

    **Impact Example:**

    On €10,000 in bookings (subscription plan):
    - Community Expert: €8,800 (after 12% fee)
    - Top Expert: €9,200 (after 8% fee)
    - **Annual Savings: €400+ per €10,000**

  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Faster Payouts">

    Access your earnings faster to manage cash flow better.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## How to Qualify

Top Expert status is earned, not purchased.

### Requirements Summary

| Criteria | Target |
|----------|--------|
| Average Rating | 4.8+ stars |
| Appointments (90 days) | 25+ completed |
| Cancellation Rate | Under 5% |
| Response Rate | 90%+ within 24hr |
| Experience | 3+ months active |
| Availability | 80%+ days active |

### Evaluation Schedule

Quarterly evaluations: January 1, April 1, July 1, October 1
