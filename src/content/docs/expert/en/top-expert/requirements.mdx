# Top Expert Requirements

Top Expert status is earned through consistent excellence. Like Airbnb's Superhost program, it recognizes professionals who deliver exceptional patient experiences.

## Overview

Top Expert is **earned, not purchased**. Qualification is based on:

- High patient satisfaction
- Consistent availability
- Reliable service delivery
- Professional communication

## Qualification Criteria

You must meet **all** of the following criteria:

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Rating Requirement">
    **Minimum 4.8 out of 5.0 stars average rating**

    - Calculated from all reviews in evaluation period
    - Responses to reviews don't affect rating
    - All reviews are weighted equally
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Appointment Volume">
    **Minimum 25 completed appointments** in the past 90 days

    - Cancelled appointments don't count
    - Patient no-shows count as completed
    - Both new and follow-up appointments count
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Cancellation Rate">
    **Less than 5% cancellation rate**

    - Measured over past 90 days
    - Only expert-initiated cancellations count
    - Patient cancellations don't affect your rate
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Response Rate">
    **90% or higher response rate** within 24 hours

    - Respond to booking inquiries
    - Answer patient messages
    - Acknowledge appointment confirmations
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Experience Requirement">
    **Minimum 3 months** as an active Community Expert

    - Profile must be published
    - Active means accepting bookings
    - Breaks don't reset the clock
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Availability Consistency">
    **Active 80% or more** of days in evaluation period

    - Days with available time slots count
    - Doesn't require bookings each day
    - Pre-marked vacation blocks don't penalize you
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>

## Evaluation Process

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Quarterly Evaluation

    Evaluations occur on January 1, April 1, July 1, and October 1.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### 90-Day Window

    Each evaluation looks at the previous 90 days of activity.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Automatic Review

    The system automatically evaluates all eligible experts. No application needed.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Results Notification

    Within 48 hours: email notification, dashboard update, and badge appears (if qualified).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Maintaining Status

<Tabs items={['Ongoing', 'Grace Period', 'Breaks']}>
  <Tab value="Ongoing">
    **Ongoing Requirements**

    Once you qualify, you must continue meeting criteria:

    - Quarterly re-evaluation
    - Same requirements each quarter
    - One "grace" quarter if you slip slightly
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Grace Period">
    **Grace Period**

    If you narrowly miss criteria:

    - One-month warning period
    - Opportunity to improve metrics
    - Full demotion if not improved

    <Callout type="warning">
      Significant violations (e.g., 15% cancellation rate, 3.5 star rating) result in immediate status loss without grace period.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Breaks">
    **Voluntary Breaks**

    Taking time off?

    1. Mark vacation in advance
    2. Notify support if extended break
    3. Status preserved for up to 30 days
    4. Longer breaks require re-qualification
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Disqualification

<Tabs items={['Automatic', 'Appeals']}>
  <Tab value="Automatic">
    **Automatic Disqualification**

    Immediate status loss for:

    - Patient safety violations
    - Policy violations
    - Verified complaints
    - Fraudulent activity
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Appeals">
    **Appeals Process**

    If you believe an evaluation was incorrect:

    1. Contact expert-support@eleva.care
    2. Provide documentation
    3. Review completed within 5 business days
    4. Decision is final
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Can I buy Top Expert status?">
    No. Top Expert status is earned exclusively through meeting performance criteria.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I'm close but don't qualify?">
    Keep working on your metrics. You'll be evaluated again next quarter.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does my subscription plan affect eligibility?">
    No. Both commission-only and subscription experts are evaluated equally.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I lose status mid-quarter?">
    Only for serious violations. Regular metrics are evaluated quarterly.
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
