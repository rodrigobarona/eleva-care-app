# Payouts & Earnings

Eleva Care uses Stripe Connect for secure, automated payouts. This guide explains how earnings work and how to maximize your income.

## How Payments Work

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Patient Books

    Payment is collected at the time of booking.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Funds Held

    Payment is held in escrow during the consultation period.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Appointment Completes

    Funds are released 24 hours after the consultation ends.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Payout Processed

    Funds are transferred to your bank account on your payout schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  The 24-hour hold after consultation allows for any disputes or issues to be resolved.
</Callout>

## Platform Fees

### Fee Structure

| Tier | Plan | Commission | Monthly Fee |
|------|------|------------|-------------|
| Community | Commission-Only | 20% | €0 |
| Community | Subscription | 12% | €49/month |
| Top Expert | Commission-Only | 15% | €0 |
| Top Expert | Subscription | 8% | €177/month |

<Tabs items={['Commission-Only', 'Subscription']}>
  <Tab value="Commission-Only">
    **Best For:**

    - Just starting out
    - Fewer than 10 bookings/month
    - Prefer no fixed costs
    - Testing the platform
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Subscription">
    **Best For:**

    - Consistent bookings
    - Want to maximize earnings per appointment
    - Prefer predictable costs
    - More than 6 bookings/month (break-even point)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Example Calculation

**€100 Consultation — Community Expert:**

| Plan | Commission | You Receive |
|------|------------|-------------|
| Commission-Only (20%) | €20 | €80 |
| Subscription (12%) | €12 | €88 |

**Break-even**: With €49/month subscription, you save €8 per booking. Break-even at ~6 bookings/month.

## Setting Up Payouts

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Start Setup

    Go to **Expert Dashboard** → **Earnings** → **Set Up Payouts**.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Complete Verification

    Complete Stripe's identity verification process.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Add Bank Account

    Provide your bank account details for receiving payments.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Await Approval

    Verification typically takes 1-2 business days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Required Information

- Government-issued ID
- Bank account details
- Tax identification number
- Business information (if applicable)

<Callout type="warning">
  You cannot receive payouts until Stripe verification is complete. Start this process early.
</Callout>

## Refunds & Cancellations

### Patient Cancellations

| Cancellation Window | Patient Refund | Your Earnings |
|---------------------|----------------|---------------|
| 48+ hours before | 100% | €0 |
| 24-48 hours before | 50% | 50% |
| Less than 24 hours | 0% | 100% |

### Your Cancellations

If you cancel an appointment:

- Patient receives full refund
- You receive no earnings
- May affect your rating and Top Expert eligibility

<Callout type="warning">
  Frequent cancellations impact your reliability score and may affect Top Expert eligibility.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Payout Not Received">
1. Check Stripe Connect status
2. Verify bank account details
3. Ensure minimum threshold met
4. Contact support if still missing
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Incorrect Amount">
Review transaction breakdown:

- Verify gross amount
- Check platform fee rate
    - Confirm any refunds applied
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Stripe Issues">
For Stripe-specific problems:

- Log into Stripe Express dashboard
- Check for verification requirements
- Update banking information if needed
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
