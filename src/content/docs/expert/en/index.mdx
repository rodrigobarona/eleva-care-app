## How It Works

### For You, the Expert

1. **Apply & Get Verified** - Submit your credentials for review
2. **Set Up Your Profile** - Create your professional profile
3. **Configure Services** - Define your consultation offerings
4. **Start Accepting Patients** - Publish your profile and begin receiving bookings

### What We Provide

- ✅ Booking and scheduling system
- ✅ Secure payment processing
- ✅ HIPAA-compliant video calls
- ✅ Patient management tools
- ✅ Earnings dashboard and payouts

### What You Provide

- ✅ Your professional expertise
- ✅ Direct patient care
- ✅ Clinical documentation (if applicable)
- ✅ Your professional judgment

<Callout type="info">
  **Important**: Eleva Care provides the technology platform. You, as an independent professional, provide the healthcare services and maintain your own malpractice insurance.
</Callout>

## Getting Started Checklist

Before you can start accepting patients, complete these steps:

- [ ] **Complete your profile** - Add your photo, bio, and credentials
- [ ] **Set your availability** - Configure your working hours
- [ ] **Create at least one service** - Define your consultation offerings
- [ ] **Connect Stripe** - Set up your payout account
- [ ] **Verify your identity** - Complete identity verification
- [ ] **Publish your profile** - Make your profile visible to patients
