## How It Works

<Tabs items={['What We Provide', 'What You Provide']}>
  <Tab value="What We Provide">
    **Platform Services**

    - Booking and scheduling system
    - Secure payment processing via Stripe
    - HIPAA-compliant video calls
    - Patient management tools
    - Earnings dashboard and payouts
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="What You Provide">
    **Your Expertise**

    - Your professional expertise and credentials
    - Direct patient care
    - Clinical documentation (if applicable)
    - Your professional judgment
    - Professional liability insurance
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout type="info">
  Eleva Care provides the technology platform. You, as an independent professional, provide the healthcare services and maintain your own malpractice insurance.
</Callout>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Complete Your Profile

    Add your photo, bio, credentials, and select your specializations.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Set Your Availability

    Configure your working hours and connect your calendar.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Create Services

    Define your consultation offerings with descriptions and pricing.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Connect Stripe

    Set up your payout account to receive earnings.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Verify Your Identity

    Complete identity and credential verification.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Publish Your Profile

    Make your profile visible to patients and start accepting bookings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
